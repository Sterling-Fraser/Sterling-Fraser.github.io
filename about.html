About

I grew up in the mountains of Colorado near South Park, and after journeying to Boulder, Colorado, I developed a passion for creating avenues to access new types of knowledge, whatever type of media it comes in. After working in the fields of documentary film, sound design, digital media, marketing, and now web design and coding, I'm continuously finding new ways to spread people's stories and ideas as wide and far as possible.
